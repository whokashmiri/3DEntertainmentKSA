# **App Name**: 3D Entertainment Co.

## Core Features:

- Homepage Hero Section: Display a large cinematic hero section featuring factory scenes, CNC machines, and fabrication processes.
- Capabilities Showcase: Showcase the company's creative design, engineering, CNC machining, fabrication, themed environments, and advanced manufacturing capabilities.
- Portfolio Page: Display completed projects with category filters, image cards, and detailed project information on hover.
- Technology and Equipment Page: Technical schematic-style layout to showcase the company's machinery, emphasizing scale, power, and precision.
- People Page: Introduce the team with a blend of human and industrial elements, team photos in dark backgrounds, and red accents.
- Contact Page: A contact form for users to request a quote or get in touch with the company.
- Dynamic Project Description Generator: An AI tool for quickly generating engaging descriptions of completed projects. The tool makes reasoning-based decisions on what design details to highlight, including materials and industry specifics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Industrial Red (#B1121A) to be used sparingly as a powerful accent.
- Background color: Matte Black (#0B0B0B) with Dark Charcoal Grey (#1E1E1E) for sections.
- Accent color: Medium Steel Grey (#6F6F6F) for secondary elements.
- Headings: 'Oswald' (sans-serif) for bold, geometric, uppercase titles with wide letter spacing. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body text: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a clean and modern look. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use red and grey icons related to machinery, fabrication, and engineering processes.
- Full-width sections with grid-based layouts and sharp edges to reflect industrial precision.
- Incorporate subtle industrial lines, separators, and technical UI details throughout the design.
- Subtle parallax, fade-in animations, and smooth hover transitions to add motion and interest.
